Public Health 142 Data Project (7/14/2020)

Patrick Udenyi, Sharicka Zutshi, David Zhong, Kayla Leiber, Edward Bian

In this project we use R to analyze the distribution of health care expenditure between different countries as a percentage of the country's total GDP. We got the data from the World Bank and used various box-plots and a QQplot to visualize the data. We also use the Kruskal Wallis test to test whether there is a significant difference in health care expenditure between high-income, medium-income, and low-income countries.
